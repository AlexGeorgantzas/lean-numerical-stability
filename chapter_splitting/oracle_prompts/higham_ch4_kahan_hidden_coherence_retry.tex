We need a Lean theorem route for Higham Chapter 4 Kahan summation.  The desired
ordinary returned-source coefficient bound is \(2u+O(nu^2)\).

Available per-step local facts for coefficients \(A,B,C,D\), with
\[
t_s=A+C,\quad t_x=B+D,\quad r=t_x-t_s,\quad c=D-C,
\]
are
\[
\begin{aligned}
|t_s-1|&\le3u^2,& |t_x-1|&\le2u+9u^2,\\
|C|&\le u(1+u)^2,& |c|&\le u(1+u)^2(3+u),\\
|c+\delta_s|&\le7u^2,& |r-c-\delta_y|&\le u^2,\\
|r+\delta_s-\delta_y|&\le8u^2,& |\delta_s|&\le u,\quad |\delta_y|\le u.
\end{aligned}
\]
Returned coordinate after a step is
\[
R(T,E)=(A+C)T+(B+D)E-(CT+DE).
\]

These facts alone are insufficient.  The two symbolic steps
\[
(A,B,C,D,\delta_s,\delta_y)=(1,1+u,0,u+u^2,-u,u),
\]
\[
(A,B,C,D,\delta_s,\delta_y)=(1+u,1+u,-u,-u,0,0)
\]
satisfy them, but a unit source injected at the first step and propagated
through the second has returned coefficient \((1+u)^3\), hence error
\(3u+3u^2+u^3\), violating \(2u+Ku^2\) for every fixed \(K\).

rules out this symbolic example and proves a \(2u+Cnu^2\) returned coefficient
bound; or
2. A corrected theorem surface with leading constant \(3\) or another constant,
plus a recurrence proof skeleton; or
3. A finite-format impossibility proof showing why the symbolic steps cannot
come from actual round-to-even Kahan operations.
