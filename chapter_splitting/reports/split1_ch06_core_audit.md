Problem 6.2 has both the rank-one operator formula and its concrete matrix transfer through a standard-basis representation of a linear functional. The local positive-value mixed subordinate norm-attainment step needed by the Theorem 6.4 sandwich argument is closed. Theorem 6.5 now has a local relative distance-to-singularity minimum theorem and a reciprocal product-form condition-number statement for the same mixed-bound predicate. Lemma 6.6(b)'s absolute-value monotonicity proof line and Lemma 6.6(d)'s first inequality are closed for the repository's rectangular 2-operator-bound predicate, with a reduction for Lemma 6.6(c) once a bound for `|B|` is supplied. Equation (6.12)'s column lower bound is closed for p=1, and equation (6.13)'s row lower bound is closed for p=∞. A lightweight concrete complex matrix-to-map bridge and local nonzero-ratio/unit-sphere maximum bridge are present, but the full concrete p-norm/supremum mixed matrix-norm and perturbation-limit condition-number API bridge is not closed. The repository also contains useful real vector/matrix 2-, 1-, infinity-, Frobenius-, and operator-2 infrastructure, but the Chapter 6 full p-norm comparison and SVD theorem surfaces are not yet closed.
