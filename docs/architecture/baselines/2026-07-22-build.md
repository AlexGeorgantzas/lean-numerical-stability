## Clean-build decision

A destructive clean-cache benchmark was not run in this migration. The
physical `Higham`-library decision is already blocked independently: the tier
inventory is intentionally partial (63 of 744 production modules classified),
eight reviewed modules remain mixed, and no package split may proceed until
classification is complete and mixed modules are eliminated. Preserving the
validated cache avoids another multi-hour rebuild while providing no evidence
that could change that decision. Run the clean benchmark when those structural
gates are satisfied and a physical split becomes an actionable proposal.
